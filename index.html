Research: Multi-stage Hydrodynamic Solver
Python C/C++ FORTRAN Matplotlib

Roles: Developer, Researcher

Description: Numerical results obtained from C/C++ solvers. Genetic algorithm in C/C++. Runs were managed by Python and Matplotlib was used for data visualization

The advection-dispersion equation with transient storage (ADE-TS) is fitted to observed stream tracer study data to elucidate stream transport characteristics. Two different numerical approaches to solving the ADE-TS are implemented. Convergence of the parameter estimation technique, a micro genetic algorithm followed by a simplex method, is demonstrated.
